Interactive Gaussian Splat 3D Viewer

Gaussian Splats are an efficient representation for rendering complex 3D scenes. Each splat is an oriented 3D ellipsoid defined by a Gaussian distribution. Splats are blended together to approximate the surface and appearance of 3D objects. This allows realistic rendering of intricate shapes with fine details.

The advantage of Gaussian Splats is that they enable real-time rendering of complex 3D scenes with a compact representation. Realistic shapes and appearances can be achieved with a relatively small number of splats compared to triangle meshes or voxel grids. This efficiency makes Gaussian Splats well-suited for interactive 3D viewing in web browsers and on mobile devices.
